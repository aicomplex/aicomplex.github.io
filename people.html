Mayank Kejriwal

Mayank Kejriwal, PhD is the director of AICS. He holds joint appointments as a research assistant professor in the University of Southern California (USC) Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, and a research team leader in the USC Information Sciences Institute. His research has been funded by the US Department of Defense (DARPA), corporations, and philanthropic foundations. He is also affiliated with the USC Center on Knowledge Graphs, and with the AI4Health initiative at ISI.


Min-Hsueh Chiu

Min-Hsueh Chiu, MSc is a research engineer in AICS. His expertise includes knowledge graphs, open-world learning, and systems engineering. He has served as key personnel in two funded projects, including the DARPA-funded Science of Artificial Intelligence Learning with Open-World Novelty (SAIL-ON), and the Healthcare Knowledge Graphs (HKG) project funded by Kaiser Permanente Digital.




Navapat Nananukul

Navapat Nananukul, MSc is a research intern in the AICS group. He received his master's from USC in Computer Science in 2023. His research interests are in large language models and knowledge graphs.




Ke Shen

Ke Shen, MSc is currently a PhD student in the USC Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, advised by Dr. Kejriwal. Her experience is in machine commonsense reasoning, especially using neurosymbolic techniques that combine knowledge graphs and semantics with large language models.




Yidan Sun

Yidan Sun, MSc is currently a PhD student in the USC Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, advised by Dr. Kejriwal. Her interests are in computational social science, visualization, and network science.


Zhisheng Tang

Zhisheng Tang, MSc is currently a PhD student in the USC Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, advised by Dr. Kejriwal. His research interests are in large language models and their emergent abilities, such as decision making and chain-of-thought reasoning.
